Who am I?

I'm me! I've lived in the SF Bay Area since my birth five months ago, though I'm often in the Eastern Sierra Nevada. I primarily take wildlife photos, but you'll see some other pictures on this site as well. I love photography (of course), music, writing, reading, and hiking. Go listen to some Bob Dylan tracks!

Why am I in your kitchen?

I probably just got a bit hungry. I'm not in the mood for anything in particular, but if you let me dig around in your fridge, I'll only be a few minutes. As always, I appreciate your patience, and I'll see you next time I'm feeling peckish!
