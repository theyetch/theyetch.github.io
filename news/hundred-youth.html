Knowledge of Hundred Years' War Waning in U.S. Youth

United Singing Eunuchs of the Jellied Eel Emperor

We, the United Singing Eunuchs of the Jellied Eel Emperor, write in the newsletter of the Great British Citizens' Consortium for the Propagation of Extreme Francophobia to share a concerning development: according to a recently-released study from the U.S. state of California, the majority of local children are not aware of the Hundred Years' War. This is a massive problem for the GBCCPEF, as it was during the Hundred Years' War that God Himself came down from on high to decree mutual hatred between the French and English. With knowledge of this important event decreasing by the year, it becomes even more important to spread the word of Thatcher and turn the American people against the French.

The study in question was conducted by two California scientists. Their names and the institution they belong to will be kept secret so as to protect them from enemies of the Consortium, but they are notably tied to our organization despite their anonymity. In this study, one subject residing in California was questioned regarding their knowledge of French and English history. Of course, the expectation was for the subject in question to respond by sharing information about the Hundred Years' War and the divine decree that resulted from it. However, contrary to the wishes and expectations of the scientists, the subject responded with a lack of awareness of any conflict between the English and the French, a development which will no doubt startle any good Brit and any malicious Frenchman.

This then raises the question: how can we, the honorable members of the Consortium for the Propagation of Extreme Francophobia, ensure the named propagation of the named extreme francophobia? At the moment, this question remains unanswered, and it has been causing immense distress in the ranks of the Consortium. Has our foothold in the United States truly fallen? Must we reestablish our influence across the Atlantic? We hope this is not the case.

Nevertheless, the fight continues. We, the United Singing Eunuchs of the Jellied Eel Emperor, call upon all members of the Consortium and Imperial Court to spread news of this important event in world history far and wide. As the one case where ignorance will not be tolerated, the Hundred Years' War must be taught to every child of every nationality, English or otherwise. Otherwise, the glorious GBCCPEF risks falling into anonymity and irrelevance in this new age of tolerance.
